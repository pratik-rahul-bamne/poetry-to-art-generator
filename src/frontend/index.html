How it works

✍️

1. Paste your poem

Type or paste any poem in English, Hindi, or Marathi. Optionally choose a theme.

🧠

2. AI analysis

Gemini Flash extracts the theme, mood, and creates a visual image prompt.

🖥️

3. Image generation

Stable Diffusion or Pollinations.ai creates a matching aesthetic background.

📥

4. Download

Get your social-media-ready image in square or story format instantly.
